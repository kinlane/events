Weather2020 offers customized forecasts for any date and location, up to 200 days out, so those planning weddings can now breathe a little easier.“We’re very excited to fnally offer tailored weather forecasts for the biggest day of most peoples’ lives,” said Chris Routh, CEO of Weather2020.“Weather is a big stress factor and giving couples an opportunity plan the outdoor wedding they’ve been dreaming about is incredible.” Over the past seven years, Weather2020 has correctly predicted numerous long-range weather forecasts, including hurricanes and other natural disasters.Weather2020 has an impressive accuracy—near 80 percent—for forecasts up to 200 days, beating conventional computer models forecasting only a week out.
