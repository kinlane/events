Kdan Mobile Software integrated the Orange Cloud API to their Pocket Scanner app to expand users storage options & facilitate use by Orange customers.Kdan’s Pocket Scanner is available worldwide via the Apple App Store.Founded in 2009, Kdan Mobile Software develops mobile apps for Apple, Android and Windows operating systems, racking up over 30 million downloads and counting over 5 million monthly active users. Not to mention, its apps have been featured on the App Store Homepage in over 80 countries.  Kdan Assistant Marketing Manager Frank Lin sat down with us to discuss Orange Cloud API integration with Pocket Scanner, the app which transforms a smartphone into a multipurpose scanner.
